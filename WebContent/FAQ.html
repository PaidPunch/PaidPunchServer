1. What is a "Punch"?

A Punch is a digital voucher of store credit that is purchased through this app using your credit card and may be used towards your purchase at the business you bought it from. Punches are sold at a very large discount (up to 90%) saving you lots of money and making it a great investment!

 

2. What is a "Mystery Punch"?

A Mystery Punch is special prize that is revealed after you use all of your regular Punches. It can be anything from a free meal to a round of drinks for you and your friends. We let the business decide what the prizes are so that they can add their own fun twist.

 

3. How do I buy Punches?

It's really easy. Press the "Buy" tab, find the business that you want to visit, and then press the "Buy Punches" button. Just enter your credit card information and whala!

 

4. Can I try this out without spending money?

Yes. The majority of businesses offer a one-time free Punch if you share a little blurb to your Facebook. They offer you this free Punch because they want you to go and try them out (which you should... because it's free and if you fall in love you can buy their Punches for even more savings!).
