It's simple.

Local businesses near you sell digital vouchers of store credit, called Punches, that you purchase in this app at a large discount (up to 90% off).

It's fun.

Every time you purchase Punches, you get a FREE Mystery Punch which can be anything from a free meal to round of drinks! Every business offers different prizes so you are always in for a fun surprise.

It's smart.

Life isn't about money, but why not save it when it's this easy?!

 

Using PaidPunch is incredibly easy, here's how it works:

 

Purchasing Punches

1. Find the Punches you want to buy.

 

2. Buy Punches using your credit card.

Your credit card information is sent over a highly secure 256-bit encrypted connection.

 

Using Punches

1. Go to My Punches.

 

2. Select an available Punch.

 

3. Show your phone to the cashier then slide.
